Chapter 58

"What are you doing!" Victoria whispered sharply, staring at him warily, "Get out now!"

Her voice was very low, afraid that people outside would hear.

William not only didn't leave immediately but instead watched with great interest.

Victoria followed his gaze and looked down-

It was a light gray bridesmaid dress, with a well-tailored, form-fitting design that highlighted her slender and delicate curves, perfectly outlining Victoria's graceful figure.

Especially the fluffy fishtail at the hem of the skirt, which added a touch of playfulness and cuteness.

Victoria was very slim, but her chest was exceptionally full, as if naturally blessed.

Her already curvaceous figure now seemed even more shapely and alluring.

"Have you seen enough!" Seeing that William's gaze hadn't moved away for a long time, Victoria was both ashamed and annoyed.

William's lips curled into a smile as he slowly approached her.

The unique male hormones instantly invaded Victoria, and she couldn't help but recall the intimate scene in the car yesterday.

His hot breath sprayed on her ear, and Victoria felt a sudden rush of sensation, as if her head was spinning.

William reached out and pinched Victoria's soft chin, forcing her to look up and meet his gaze.

Victoria's eyes were moist and sparkling.

William felt his heart skip a beat.

He leaned down and kissed Victoria's red lips, his warm and slippery tongue prying open her teeth, greedily savoring the sweet taste in her mouth.

Victoria was kissed until she was dizzy, almost losing her soul.

When she came to her senses, she immediately struggled.

"No!"

William grabbed Victoria's arm and pulled her into his arms.

His other hand clasped her slender waist, pressing her tightly against his chest.

Victoria's suppressed breathing turned into intermittent moans, sounding particularly subtle in the quiet room.

William pressed her against the wall, his large hand slowly moving down along her elegant collarbone.

A tingling sensation rose from her spine, and Victoria couldn't help but moan softly.

"Don't move!" William warned deeply, continuing to tease her.

"Stop, stop."

"If you move again, I'll make love to you right here." William threatened.

Victoria bit her lip, not daring to move.

She knew that if she dared to struggle again, William would really make love to her right here!

She closed her eyes in despair.

William's hand had already moved up from her thigh, and a cold touch made her shiver.

"No." Victoria pleaded, her voice already tinged with sobs, "Not here, there are people outside."

William ignored her and continued his actions.

Finally, William's hand touched Victoria's private part, and Victoria trembled all over, involuntarily clamping her legs.

William took the opportunity to explore, lingering in her depths.

Victoria was stimulated to the point of whimpering.

"Not here." She bit her lower lip hard, trying not to make any shameful sounds.

"Relax, I won't go in, just playing around."

His tone was extremely teasing.

Victoria bit her lip and didn't make a sound.

At this moment, the voice of a store clerk came from outside.

"Ms. Carter, do you need any help?"

Hearing someone knocking on the door, Victoria felt like she was reborn. She used all her strength to push William away, quickly tidied herself up, and opened the door as if nothing had happened.

The store clerk was stunned when she saw her.

Victoria's cheeks were flushed, and her eyes were evasive.

William, without any concealment, swaggered out of the fitting room as well. He slightly raised his chin, not even looking at the store clerk, and walked straight to the sofa to sit down.

The store clerk stood there awkwardly.

Seeing the two of them like this, what else was there not to understand?

She just didn't expect an affair to be so blatant.

She wisely pretended not to see anything.

Pulling out an awkward smile, she said, "Ms. Carter, Ms. Garcia's makeup is done. Do you want to try the bridesmaid makeup?"

"Okay." Victoria coughed lightly, covering the blush on her face, and followed the store clerk out.

William also got up and followed.

Ella stood on a round display platform, her wedding dress inlaid with countless diamonds, dazzling and eye-catching.

The hem of the wedding dress was embroidered with intricate patterns in silver thread, each pattern exquisite and elegant, luxurious and grand.

Ella wore delicate makeup, her hair styled into a beautiful bun, and with her bright face, it was hard to look away.

Even Victoria, who disliked her, had to admit that Ella in a wedding dress was indeed stunning.

Seeing Victoria and William come in together, Ella's originally smiling face instantly turned cold. She snorted lightly and turned her head away.

William stepped forward, extending his right arm to embrace her shoulder, "Very beautiful."

Ella was delighted to hear this, and her earlier displeasure vanished instantly.

She looked up at William, coquettishly asking, "William, how is it?"

William's lips curled into a doting smile, "Very good, choose this one, and pick a few more as backups."

Ella beamed, affectionately holding William's arm, ignoring Victoria on the side. .

After all, she was about to become the rightful Mrs. Scott, so why bother with a lowly mistress.

As they left the bridal shop, Ella wanted to ride in the same car as William, but William refused.

"Be good, I have to go back to the company for some work. You go home and rest first." William patted her cheek, smiling as he instructed.

Ella glanced at Victoria, although unwilling, she didn't say anything.

She obediently got into her car, and as the door closed, the smile on her face instantly disappeared, replaced by a heavy gloom.

That shameless bitch.

Ella floored the gas pedal, the roaring engine speeding past William and Victoria, kicking up a cloud of dust.

Victoria covered her nose, frowning as she glared in the direction Ella left.

William fanned the dust away and asked Victoria, "Going home? I'll take you."

Victoria shook her head, "No need, I can go back myself."

"Really?" William raised an eyebrow, his tone rising, "Are you angry?"

"I'm not angry." Victoria denied flatly.

"Then get in the car." William insisted.

Seeing William's firm attitude, Victoria hesitated for a moment, just about to agree, when a dark blue Bentley suddenly drove up, braking sharply in front of them.

The window slowly rolled down, revealing a handsome profile.

It was Samuel.
