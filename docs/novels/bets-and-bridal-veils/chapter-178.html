Chapter 178

Last night, Michael was still whispering sweet nothings to her in bed, but as soon as he woke up, he couldn't wait to meet Violet.

It seems those reporters outside had a reason to be there.

Michael is a flirt to the core.

Luckily, she wasn't completely fooled by his facade, saying things like "leave everything to me," how ridiculous.

Men, none of them are trustworthy.

Isabella suppressed her anger and put on a perfect smile, "So the person who had an appointment with Ms. Cooper is Mr. Brown?"

"Now it seems my appearance has disturbed your pleasant time!"

She squinted her eyes, looking Michael and Violet up and down, appearing calm on the surface but feeling like she was about to explode inside.

But to Michael, Isabella's expression seemed like she didn't care about him at all.

When did he say he was dating Violet?

She clearly promised him last night that she would trust him unconditionally, is this how she trusts him?

Even if she showed a hint of anger, he would be happy.

But she never cared about him.

Michael smiled bitterly, in the end, it was all just his wishful thinking, Isabella didn't care about him, so why would she care about him now.

The tension between them grew stronger, making Violet seem like an outsider.

She calmly spoke in an innocent tone, "Ms. Taylor, I'm really sorry, it was my words that caused the misunderstanding."

"Mr. Brown came to visit the school today, and the teachers asked me to accompany him. At this time, I thought it wouldn't be good for Mr. Brown to dine alone, so I decided to keep him company."

As Violet spoke, she kept glancing at Michael with undisguised love and affection in her eyes.

Isabella felt even angrier.

They were so blatant, did she need Violet to come over and explain their date details?

Especially Michael, who didn't say a word.

Wasn't he feeling guilty!

"Ms. Cooper, you misunderstood. My relationship with him ended long ago. When the time comes, we'll get divorced. He can be with whoever he wants, date whoever he wants, I couldn't care less."

Hearing this, Michael's face immediately darkened.

Divorce when the time comes?

She had planned all this out, didn't she? Did she not even care about Daniel?

But hearing this made Violet secretly happy, unable to hide the smugness on her face.

Seeing this, Isabella couldn't help but raise an eyebrow and deliberately said, "I almost forgot to tell Ms. Cooper, the person Michael likes is named Bianca, my half-sister. You, at best, are just a temporary interest for him."

"Maybe he'll get tired of you in a few days, so don't get your hopes up too high. After all, a man's heart changes quickly."

Isabella's words were a thorough scolding of Michael.

But at this moment, Isabella's eyes were fixed on Violet's reaction.

No matter how upset she was, she didn't forget her purpose for coming here.

If Violet was Bianca, she couldn't have no reaction upon hearing Bianca's name.

But strangely, when Violet heard these words, she instinctively glanced at Michael, with deep envy in her eyes.

"So Ms. Bianca Taylor is the one Mr. Brown truly likes. She must be very beautiful and gentle, otherwise, she wouldn't have won Mr. Brown's love."

Isabella frowned.

Violet's reaction was too calm, not like she was pretending.

This wasn't the result she wanted, maybe Violet wasn't Bianca?

Regardless, she couldn't get the answer here now.

Looking at the tall and silent Michael in front of her, Isabella was very angry and turned to leave.

She feared she would start cursing if she stayed another second.

Watching Isabella leave, Michael's eyes deepened, but he didn't follow.

Seeing this, Violet felt even happier.

She outwardly advised Michael, "Mr. Brown, Ms. Taylor has left, aren't you going to chase after her?"

Michael's eyes darkened, and he spoke with a hint of amusement, "Ms. Cooper, are you willing to push me away? Didn't you say you wanted to play the piano for me, that's why I'm here?"

Isabella could think of this, how could he not?

Isabella left angrily, and as soon as she reached the first floor, she received a call from Nolan.

Thinking about how she left without a word yesterday because of Daniel, Isabella quickly answered the call.

Nolan's deep and pleasant voice came through the phone, "Isabella, I heard from the students that you're at the school?"

"Yes."

She was planning to find Nolan.

"Nolan, where are you, in your office?"

"No, I'm in the music room, are you coming over?"

Nolan's tone was expectant.

"Okay."

Isabella agreed decisively, asked for the location, and went over.

When she reached the door, she heard Nolan talking, with two students beside him asking questions.

When Nolan looked over unintentionally, he met Isabella's eyes.

They smiled at each other.

The students beside them nodded and thanked Nolan before leaving.

As they passed by Isabella, they smiled ambiguously.

It made her a bit embarrassed.

At this moment, Nolan, sitting in a wheelchair, spoke, "Isabella, why are you at the school today? I remember the school didn't schedule any classes for you, right?"

This question made Isabella pause.

After a while, she found a not-so-reasonable reason, "I was bored at home, so I came over to take a walk and see my future students."

She couldn't say she came specifically to investigate his student Violet.

And accidentally ran into Michael here.

The most frustrating part was that Violet and her nominal husband Michael were on a date at this moment.

Just hearing it made her scalp tingle.

She better not tell Nolan.

Nolan is such a clean person, saying it would only dirty his ears.

Nolan smiled and shook his head.

Clearly, he didn't believe that the head of the Wilson Group would leisurely come to the school for a walk and a look around.

Moreover, the school was full of students, secrets couldn't be hidden.

Nolan casually asked, "Isabella, did you know Michael was here, so you came over?"

Isabella was stunned, avoiding Nolan's deep eyes.

"How could that be? Why would I come to find him? I just came to look around."

Before coming, she didn't know Michael was here.

After arriving at the school, she found out Michael had come to find Violet, even to the school.

She should have known he wouldn't change his nature, she wouldn't have believed a word he said.

"Alright, you two haven't divorced yet, what's there to hide between a couple, have you learned to lie?"

Nolan had an "I understand" expression.

After all, Michael's fame was bigger than a celebrity's, his appearance caused a sensation in the whole school.
